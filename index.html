ESNConnect
Edmonton Shelter Network

A service coordination system for frontline workers.

ESN Connect allows shelter staff and frontline social service workers to create, look up, and share client records across the Edmonton Shelter Network. It is built around the ESN ID card, a shelter-issued credential that gives unhoused people a stable identity within the service system, even without government-issued photo ID.

This is a demonstration prototype built for RSCH3000 Applied Research. No real client data is stored or transmitted.

  • Create new client records and generate ESN ID cards
  • Look up existing clients and view their service history
  • Coordinate referrals across participating organizations
  • Access the Edmonton agency directory
  • Read the funding problem explainer
